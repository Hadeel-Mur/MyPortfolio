The concept combines the antiquity of the oldest continuously written and spoken language of the Middle East and the depth and vastness of the ocean. I was inspired by the combination of the Aramaic alphabet, the massive structure of the coral reef, and nature’s curvy lines. Considering that the waves are the foundation of marine life, The beautiful elements that the waves and the letters share, I illustrated the idea of the coral reef in the presented shapes. The sculptures present the harmony between all of these reflections. Nature’s curvy lines are one of the perfect guidelines to mirror the details of living creatures, and for that reason, they are a fundamental combination in my project. These are the elements that I wanted to link and indicate in my project. In conclusion, The shapes also represent the balance between mass and space.

My challenge for this project was to explore how you can create an elastic shape invented from a solid material through a simulation and contrast between mass and space, The contradiction of a rough surface and the smoothness of the presented body, and abstract shapes.
